ABOUT US

We are glad to introduce ourselves as an International source of Indian Spices, Oil Seeds, Edible Oils,  Dehydrated Vegetables, Pickles, Flours, Grains &

Beans, Groceries and Various Indian Food Stuffs. We are a bridge between the international buyers and supply source of India in our own “BTS” Brand. We

procure the best quality from our genuine suppliers to maintain the same taste to our reputed customers and end users. We have marked our resence in

over 07 countries worldwide.

We ensure maximum customer satisfaction by providing high quality spices and other food products as per their particular requirements. We are fully

committed to supply pure and hygienically cleaned products to our customer worldwide at competitive price.

We are the growing player in the contemporary market of agro products as well as other food stuffs. In this dynamic global market our organizational goals

remain unchanged.
